Welcome To Hiking Bar Ella

Nestled in the heart of Ella, Sri Lanka, our bar and restaurant offer a perfect blend of delicious cuisine, live music, and breathtaking mountain views. Whether you're unwinding after a trek to Nine Arch Bridge or Little Adam's Peak, our vibrant atmosphere welcomes both tourists and locals.

Join us to savor mouth-watering Sri Lankan specialties, international dishes, refreshing drinks, and the best live music Ella has to offer. Experience authentic hospitality in the stunning highlands of Sri Lanka.
